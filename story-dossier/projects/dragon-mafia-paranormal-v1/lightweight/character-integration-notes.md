## SERA VOLKOV - Integration Enhancements

### 1. Storm Dragon Affinity (Elemental Manifestation)

**What Was Added:**
- Specific manifestations of Storm dragon powers: electronics malfunction when she's angry (phones die, surveillance cameras glitch, lights flicker), static electricity generation when furious/aroused, danger-sensing like sensing incoming weather
- Constant need to control emotions to maintain cover in Kael organization
- Early-warning system that's kept her alive but is exhausting to manage

**Why This Matters:**
- Creates FRICTION: She has to suppress her natural affinity while undercover, making every emotional moment dangerous
- Ties her powers to the world's magic system (elemental affinities as described in world doc)
- Creates romantic tension: her affinity betrays her attraction to Darius (static sparks, electronic malfunctions)
- Makes her Storm dragon heritage VISIBLE and CONSEQUENTIAL rather than just a label

**Integration Achievement:** Her character trait (control freak) now has a LITERAL manifestation (must control emotions or expose herself). This makes psychological traits and magical powers reinforce each other.

**Why This Matters:**

- Makes her Volkov heritage a TOOLKIT not just backstory—she has specific skills from specific training

**Integration Achievement:** Her professional skills are now inseparable from her clan's enterprise and her dragon affinity. Storm = revelation, Volkov = truth-seeking, Sera's training = specialized verification techniques. All layers reinforce the core theme.
